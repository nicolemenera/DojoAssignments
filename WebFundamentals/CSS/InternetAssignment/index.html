Clients and Services

Computers connected to the Web are called clients and servers.

  • Clients are the typical Web user's Internet-connected devices (for example, your computer connected to your Wi-Fi, or your phone connected to your mobile network) and Web-accessing software available on those devices (usually a web browser like Firfox or Chrome).
  • Servers are computers that store webpages, sites, or apps. When a client device wants to acccess a webpage, a copy of the webpage is downloaded from the server onto the client machine to be displayed in the user's web browser.
